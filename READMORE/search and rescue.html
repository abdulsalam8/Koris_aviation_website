Search And Rescue

From high profile disaster recovery and rescue missions to co-ordination of relief efforts, our UAVs are ideally suited to support customers in civilian and security agencies tasked with implementation of Search & Rescue/Relief missions

Balogun Market Fire

Firefighter Drones are sent to fire locations as scouts, equipped with thermal sensors, which use infrared radiation to help first responders locate heat signatures of humans and fire hotspots that show where fires are most likely to spread. Drones help you decide within minutes the type and amount of resources to send to a fire scene for Scene Monitoring, Search and Rescue, Post fire or Disaster, Assessment and Wildland firefighting.

Lokoja Flood Plain

In flood management, drones are veritable tools for quick and objective real-time monitoring of rescue efforts. Drones map the flooded area and detect trapped people, water leakage and flood barriers at dams. The high resolution imagery and precise data are essential for flood management.
