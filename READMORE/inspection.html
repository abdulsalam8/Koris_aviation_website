Inspections of Critical Infrastructure

Many organisations have invested heavily in deploying large infrastructure across expansive geographic network. This Infrastructure must be routinely inspected to ensure integrity and safety. Traditional inspections by deploying people often entail high risk and long journeys. We provide a range of unmanned aerial vehicles to take on this task.

Power-lines

Power grids often span across far distances, which can be hard to access therefore manual inspections can be time-consuming. Also, defects high up on the assets could be overlooked when observing from the ground. Drones can take off in minutes, covering large areas during each flight, delivering actionable insights efficiently. Drones help identify risks on asset integrity and inform response action by providing close-up data as well as comprehensive overviews.

Oil Pipelines

Oil and Gas industry is highly hazardous, and preventing malfunctions can save a lot of lives, therefore regular inspections are important. Traditional methods including foot, vehicle and manned aircraft are slow, expensive and dangerous to conduct. Drones are cost-effective platforms for oil and gas pipeline inspections due to their range and ease of operations. Using drones provides a number of advantages, including ability to maintain and operate a large fleet of aerial platforms, reduce cost of operation, faster turnaround time, flexibility to perform a variety of roles, higher quality inspection due to its ability to fly closer to the source and safety of operation.

Railways

We offer a cutting edge Drone solution for the Railway industry, including Surveillance and Aerial Inspections of Infrastructure and Assets to prevent catastrophic damage. Drones are employed to monitor significant railway infrastructures such as high-voltage electrical lines, railway catenary lines, tracks and switching points. With drone technology, extensive imagery is obtained to magnify the process of defect detections, crack detections and potential dangers quickly.

Roads in Nigeria

Drones are advanced tools for management of roads and bridges. Besides Surveying and Mapping of new and existing roads faster; Aerial inspections of projects and defect detection such as potholes and cracks, Alignment of Divider, Over line structures, Boundaries, Electric lines for Street Lights, Expansion of roads, Power lines crossings are more efficient.

Mobile Phone Towers

Communication towers are generally tall and complex structures that require unique and specialist approach to inspection. With drone technology, communication and cell towers can be inspected with Higher-resolution imagery, reduced man hour and cost, safely and efficiently.
